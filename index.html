Welcome to KR LAB Bio

One health: The best health for people, animals, and environment

About us

One health aims to bring healthy people, healthy animals, and healthy environment together. Our research focuses:

  • Comparative medicine: A study of similarities and differences between human and veterinary medicine
  • Antibiotic resistance - esp. multiple drug resistance
  • Zoonotic diseases and one health approach

Services we offer

Our services are based on the high-throughput molecular technologies and associated bioinformatics which were dramatically developed recently.

  • NGS data and genomics data analysis
  • Human or animal-derived microbiota analysis
  • Consolidation and visualization of of experimental data and omics data
  • Multi-omics data integration and interpretation
  • Monitoring disease and health status of people, animals, and enviroment
  • Diagnosis and monitoring kit for pets and family
